How to Prepare for a Hurricane

Make a Plan

Create an evacuation plan and ensure all family members know the route and emergency contacts.

Emergency Kit

Prepare a kit with non-perishable food, water, first aid, flashlights, hygiene items, documents, and medications.

Secure Your Home

Install storm shutters or board up windows. Secure outdoor items and reinforce doors if needed.

Know Your Evacuation Zone

Check with local authorities to know your evacuation zone and where to go if you need to evacuate.

Monitor Weather Alerts

Stay informed through NOAA Weather Radio, TV, or mobile apps to track the stormâ€™s progress.

During the Storm

Stay indoors, away from windows. Use a generator only outside, and avoid floodwaters.

After the Storm

Check for injuries and property damage. Avoid floodwaters and report downed power lines to authorities.
