Honduras

Honduras is a vibrant Central American country with a population of around 10 million. Its capital is Tegucigalpa, and Spanish is the official language. The country boasts diverse geography, with mountains, rainforests, and coastlines on both the Caribbean Sea and the Pacific Ocean. Honduran culture is a mix of indigenous, Spanish, and African influences, reflected in its music, dance, and festivals.
