Hurricanes and Their Impact on Land

Overview

This website provides an in-depth look at hurricanes, covering their formation, impacts on land, and safety measures. It serves as an educational resource for those living in hurricane-prone areas or anyone interested in understanding these natural phenomena.

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to inform visitors about hurricanes, emphasizing their effects on both natural and human environments. It also aims to offer practical advice for preparedness and safety.
